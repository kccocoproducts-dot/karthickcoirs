Nursery Cocopeat

Nursery Cocopeat is a lightweight, fine-textured growing medium specially prepared for seed trays, polybags and young plants.

It provides excellent moisture retention and aeration, which improves germination rate, root development and plant survival.

Applications

  • Seed trays
  • Polybags
  • Saplings
  • Horticulture nurseries
  • Home gardening

Benefits

  • High water retention
  • Excellent aeration
  • Disease-free medium
  • Promotes strong roots

Packing

  • 5 Kg compressed blocks
  • Loose cocopeat
