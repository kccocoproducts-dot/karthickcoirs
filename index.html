Our Products

Low EC Cocopeat (5 Kg Blocks & Loose)

Low EC cocopeat is washed and buffered to remove excess salts, making it ideal for sensitive crops and export use. It has excellent water retention, high porosity and uniform texture.

  • Suitable for nurseries, greenhouse & vegetables
  • Promotes strong root growth
  • Export & India supply available

High EC Cocopeat (5 Kg Blocks & Loose)

High EC cocopeat is a natural and economical growing medium used mainly for soil conditioning and non-sensitive crops. It improves soil aeration and moisture retention.

  • Cost effective
  • Ideal for landscaping and farming
  • Bulk supply available

Cocopeat Grow Bags

Cocopeat grow bags are ready-to-use cultivation media filled with premium cocopeat, used in greenhouse and terrace farming.

  • Strong UV-stabilized bags
  • Excellent water retention
  • Suitable for vegetables & flowers

Poultry Coco Pith

Coco pith is an excellent poultry bedding material due to its high absorbency and hygiene. It keeps poultry floors dry and controls odor.

  • Highly absorbent
  • Eco-friendly and biodegradable
  • Ideal for broiler & layer farms

Nursery Cocopeat

Nursery cocopeat is lightweight, porous and ideal for seedlings and young plants. It helps healthy root development and improves plant survival.

  • Used in seed trays and polybags
  • Retains moisture and nutrients
  • Perfect for nurseries
