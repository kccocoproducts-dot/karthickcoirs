High EC Cocopeat

High EC Cocopeat is natural, unwashed cocopeat produced from coconut husk pith. It contains higher mineral and salt content, making it suitable for soil conditioning, landscaping and hardy crops.

This cocopeat improves soil aeration, water retention and root penetration. It is widely used as a cost-effective organic soil conditioner for agriculture, gardening and plantation crops.

Applications

  • Landscaping and gardening
  • Open field farming
  • Tree plantations
  • Ornamental plants

Benefits

  • Improves soil structure
  • Enhances root growth
  • Retains moisture
  • Eco-friendly

Packing

  • 5 Kg compressed blocks
  • Loose cocopeat
