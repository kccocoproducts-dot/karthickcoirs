Poultry Coco Pith

Poultry Coco Pith is a highly absorbent bedding material made from coconut husk pith. It is widely used in broiler and layer farms to keep poultry floors dry and hygienic.

It absorbs moisture quickly, controls odor and reduces bacterial growth, creating a healthier environment for birds and reducing disease risk.

Benefits

  • Very high moisture absorption
  • Odor control
  • Keeps floors dry
  • Eco-friendly and biodegradable

Applications

  • Broiler farms
  • Layer farms
  • Hatcheries

Packing

  • Loose coco pith
  • Compressed bales
