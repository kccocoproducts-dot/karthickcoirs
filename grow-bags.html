Cocopeat Grow Bags

Cocopeat Grow Bags are ready-to-use cultivation bags filled with premium quality cocopeat. They are specially designed for greenhouse, polyhouse and terrace farming.

These grow bags provide excellent drainage, moisture retention and strong root support, resulting in higher crop yield and healthier plants.
