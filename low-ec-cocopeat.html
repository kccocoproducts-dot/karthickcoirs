Low EC Cocopeat

Low EC Cocopeat is washed and buffered to remove excess salts and impurities. It is ideal for sensitive crops, nurseries and export quality farming. It offers excellent water retention and air porosity which supports strong root growth.

Applications

  • Greenhouse & polyhouse farming
  • Vegetable and flower cultivation
  • Nurseries and seed trays
  • Hydroponics

Packing

  • 5 Kg compressed blocks
  • Loose cocopeat
  • Export pallet packing available
