Low EC Cocopeat

Low EC Cocopeat is a premium washed and buffered growing medium produced from natural coconut husk. It is specially processed to remove excess salts and impurities, making it ideal for sensitive crops and export farming.

Our Low EC cocopeat has excellent water holding capacity, high porosity and uniform structure. This allows plant roots to breathe better and absorb nutrients efficiently.

Applications

  • Greenhouse & polyhouse cultivation
  • Nurseries and seedling trays
  • Vegetables, flowers and fruits
  • Export quality farming

Available Forms

  • 5 Kg blocks
  • Loose cocopeat
