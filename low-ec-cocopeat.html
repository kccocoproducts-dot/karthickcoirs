Low EC Cocopeat

Low EC Cocopeat is washed and buffered to remove excess salts. It is ideal for export quality farming, greenhouse cultivation, nurseries and sensitive crops.

Technical Specifications

  • EC: Below 0.5
  • pH: 5.5 – 6.5
  • Moisture: Below 15%
  • Expansion: 70 – 75 liters per 5kg block

Packing

  • 5kg Blocks
  • Loose Cocopeat
