About Us

Karthick Coirs is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality cocopeat and coir products based in Palani, Tamil Nadu. We supply premium Low EC and High EC cocopeat for agriculture, poultry farms, and nurseries, supporting both domestic and export markets.

Our Products

  • Low EC Cocopeat – Washed & buffered, ideal for nurseries, vegetables, flowers, and export use.
  • High EC Cocopeat – Economical option suitable for soil conditioning and non-sensitive crops.
  • Cocopeat Grow Bags – Ready-to-use grow bags for terrace farming and greenhouse cultivation.
  • Coco Pith for Poultry Farms – Highly absorbent bedding material to keep floors dry and hygienic.
  • Cocopeat for Nursery – Lightweight growing media for seedlings, saplings, and polybags.
